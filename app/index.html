STEM FEM
Encouraging Females in Middle and High School to Learn More About STEM!

ABOUT US

The Stem Fem Site acts as a resource hub for girls to check out activities like STEM camps and events that align with their interests and our fit for their age group. Stay in the know with our STEM loop. Learn about hackathons, mentorship programs, large tech companies and more opportunites that girls like you have shared. Explore featured organizations created by girls for girls. Get involved in their team, participate in their opportunities, or hit them with a follow on Instagram. Let's create a community!

OUR MISSION

STEM FEM is a platform created with the goal of bridging the STEM gender gap by providing as many resources as we can to all pre-college students interested in the field. Our page provides information about free STEM camps, mentorships, events, Hackathons, and internships to give opportunities and instill confidence in young women to achieve anything they set their mind to, regardless of their financial situation.
