Take Action

How can you make a difference?

Be a part of the movement by supporting existing organizations that empower and offer opportunities to girls in STEM.

Action Item List

Mark the boxes that you've completed to track your girls in STEM journey.

  • 5. Join a grassroots, student-led girls in STEM organization or participate in events they lead
