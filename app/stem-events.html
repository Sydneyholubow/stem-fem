Free Conferences and Events 2021

Superposition Superposition is an international non-profit bridging the gender gap in STEM. Our mission is to empower women in STEM by creating educational opportunities & supportive spaces for developing their passions. They provide year-round opportunities for our community through a webinar series, Uber mentorship program, chapter program, and more.


Built by Girls Built by Girls hosts events across the country for female and non-binary students to learn about what’s needed to make their first career move, together. Get a peek into a tech company’s office at an Inside Look, learn how to network, and make power moves all day at their annual Summit.


Girl Genius Girl Genius fosters an inclusive community where everyone is given a voice to empower the next generation of female leaders in STEAM. They are confident that anyone can pursue careers in STEAM and challenge conventional gender standards. Check out their magazine and upcoming workshops and conferences.
