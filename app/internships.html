Fred Hutchinson Cancer Research Center Program is a Summer High School Internship Program designed for students from underrepresented backgrounds in biomedical science: 8-week paid internship for high school students passionate about science. Program includes one week of hands-on training in the lab and seven weeks in a faculty member’s research lab. Interns will attend weekly workshops and present their work during the final week.
