Hackathons

Female Geared Hackathons 2021

Hack Violet (Jan 30-31) is a 24-hour female-empowerment hackathon with a specific mission to encourage all individuals to learn about and become change agents for the gender gap in technology by innovating and inspiring with code. They believe in the value of hosting events that bring together people of all genders under a common goal: to make tech an inclusive space that celebrates women (trans and cis) and other minorities.


Pearl Hacks (Feb 19-21) is an annual 48-hour hackathon for women and non-binary students, organized at UNC Chapel Hill. They cater to first-time hackers who are looking for a supportive environment to explore technology! There are tons of workshops about coding, women in STEM panels, and team formation events. No coding experience required.


Women In Computer Science Hackathon (Feb 20-21) is hosting their fifth annual hackathon with both beginner and intermediate technical workshops in the days leading up to the hackathon and a 24-hour hackathon starting on Saturday! They also have various company talks and time for you to meet our sponsors. WiCS Hacks is open to ALL women and non-binary students in any major. All skill levels are welcome and we are beginner-friendly!
